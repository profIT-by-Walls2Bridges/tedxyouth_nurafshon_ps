Our Goals

Importance of the Event

Organizing a TEDxYouth event is driven by the conviction that young voices possess unique insights and untapped potential. By providing a platform for these voices, we aim to inspire curiosity, foster innovation, and empower the next generation of thought leaders. The goal is to spark meaningful conversations and encourage young minds to dream big and actively contribute to shaping a better future.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

Meet Our Team

We are the dynamic organizing team of TEDxNurafshon Presidental School Youth event, a harmonious blend of creativity, efficiency, and collaboration. With meticulous planning and seamless coordination, we curate engaging and thought-provoking experiences that leave a lasting impact. Our passion for ideas worth spreading drives us to execute flawlessly, turning each TEDx event into a transformative and memorable journey for our audience.
